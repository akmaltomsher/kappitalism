Transforming industries with visionary leadership
About

I believe better systems create better lives. That belief has shaped my journey, from my military service to tech innovation and policy reform.

Thats why I have built systems like Xplor, Kismet, Dwell and The After Party: My mission is to solve real-world challenges and improve how people live, work and access care.

Xplor gives back hundreds of hours each year, so educators can stop managing paperwork and start nurturing minds.
The average Australian needs over 10 years to save for a house deposit. Dwell asks - why should you do it alone?
Kismet automates the admin, so families dont have to choose between earning an income and looking after loved ones.
01

Technology & Education

Empowering families and educators with purpose-built tech

Weve seen firsthand how outdated systems hold back our schools, centres and communities. From childcare to the classroom, were using technology to put time back in the hands of educators and connection back at the core of learning. Our tools are built by people whove worked the frontlines; streamlining admin, improving communication and making sure families come first.

02

Reform Healthcare

Shifting aged care from transactional to human-centred

Our care systems are broken; fragmented, impersonal and riddled with red tape. We're redesigning them around the people who need it. Through AI-powered tools and human-first design, were making it easier for carers and families to focus on what truly matters: connection, dignity and better outcomes across every stage of life.

03

Drive Growth

Driving policies that support dignity, housing and equity for all

A thriving economy isnt just about numbers - its about people having a roof over their heads, a stake in their future and a chance to contribute. Were designing long-term strategies that deliver real returns: Affordable housing, future-proof jobs and a system that gives every generation a fair go.
